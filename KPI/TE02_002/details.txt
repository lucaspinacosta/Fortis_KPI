Smart KPI

    Real-time data collection with latency < 150 ms

Feature
    The firmware must provide real-time control capabilities over ROS to ensure the accurate and timely execution of tasks.


Conclusion:
    The firmware satisfies the “Real-time data collection with latency < 150 ms” KPI for the joints position topic. Messages are published at an average interval of ~100 ms (10 Hz), with all observed intervals between 96 ms and 103 ms (peak-to-peak jitter 7 ms, 1-σ jitter ≈ 1.6 ms), below the 150 ms threshold.

Methodology: 
    1. Launched the firmware and ROS master with the telehandler joint‐state publisher active.  
    2. Ran:
        rostopic hz /ugv0/telehandler/joints
    3. Collected a window of 120 samples to compute:
    - Average publish rate (Hz) → average period (ms)  
    - Minimum interval  
    - Maximum interval  
    - Standard deviation of intervals  
    4. Verified that under these nominal conditions all intervals stayed < 150 ms.
